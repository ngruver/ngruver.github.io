I am currently researching topics in control, machine learning, and convex optimization with Professor Stephen Boyd at Stanford University, working towards the Ph.D. degree in electrical engineering. I received the M.S. degree in electrical engineering from Stanford University, in 2019, and the B.S. degree in electrical engineering and computer science from the University of California, Berkeley, in 2017. Previously, I have interned at Lyft Level 5, Google (in particular, Skybox Imaging/Terra Bella), Qualcomm-Atheros, and SoRoCo.
